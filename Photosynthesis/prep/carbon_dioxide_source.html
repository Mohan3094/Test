Preparation of the carbon-dioxide source:

- Add 1 g of baking soda to 100 mL of water and mix the solution well. Add 3 - 5 drops of liquid dishwasher soap to this and mix well.

- Prepare this solution in a beaker (beaker A).

- The purpose of the liquid detergent is to break the hydrophobic layer on the leaf surface. This enables the entry of bicarbonate molecules into the plant (in between the plant cells).
